============================== Custom Design Process ==============================

Stop by, email, or call with your ideas, and one of our CAD designers will guide you through the process.

Our designers will then create a CAD model. You will be sent images of the piece to review and make adjustments if necessary.

Once approved, we will create a wax model and cast in any metal of your choosing.

After the piece has been cast, our jewelers will polish, set, and hand-engrave the piece as desired.

=================== CAD modeling and design ===================

Our talented CAD designers can replicate any sketch, image, or idea. We will work with you through the design process and create a perfect model to fit your customer's needs.

We also accept .STL and .3dm files for model building on any of our rapid prototyping machines.

================== 3d Printing ==================

We use the latest in 3d printing technology from Solidscape, Projet, and EnvisionTEC 3d printers, as well as the Revo and Roland mills. Each machine has its strengths and limitations and we select the best machine based on level of detail, fragility of the part, complexity of the geometry, and size of the part. We are capable of creating any jewelry, down to 25 micron resolution for the finest detail, to the largest pendants.
